  • 1. The background should preferably be a plain wall
  • 2. Only one person should be visible to the camera
  • 3. If you use specticles please do not wear them while clicking the picture
  • 4. There should be enough light reflecting your face
  • 5. Allow this website to access your Camera
  • 6. Look into the Camera
  • 7. Keep your face straight
